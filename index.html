How It Works

A seamless four-step pipeline from idea to published TikTok content.

1

AI Generation

Our AI engine writes motivational scripts, selects visuals, and generates voiceovers tailored to the creator's niche.

2

Smart Clipping

Long-form content is automatically cut into TikTok-optimized short clips with captions, transitions, and branding overlays.

3

Postiz Scheduling

Clips are handed to Postiz, where the user sets an optimal posting schedule. Our app auto-posts directly to TikTok at the right time.

4

Postiz Analytics

Postiz pulls TikTok engagement stats — likes, followers, views — to build performance dashboards and guide future content strategy.

TikTok API Permissions Required

The following scopes are requested under our TikTok Developer application. Each scope is used for a specific, user-facing feature described below. No scope is requested beyond what is strictly necessary.

user.info.basic

READ

Fields accessed: open_id, avatar_url, display_name

Used to create a personalized dashboard in our app and Postiz, display the user's TikTok avatar and name, and link their TikTok account to our platform securely. The open_id serves as the unique identifier to associate the TikTok account with the user's Zalyshok profile without storing any sensitive credentials.

video.publish

WRITE CORE FEATURE

Action: Directly publish video to user's TikTok profile

Essential for our core auto-posting feature. It allows our platform to publish the AI-generated motivational clips automatically at times scheduled by the user via Postiz. The user explicitly sets their posting schedule in Postiz and grants consent at the time of account connection. No content is ever published without the user's prior scheduling and approval.

video.upload

WRITE

Action: Send content to creator's TikTok account as a draft

Allows users to send generated clips as drafts to their TikTok app, giving them the flexibility to add native trending sounds, filters, or stickers before manually publishing. This scope serves users who prefer a manual review step before going live, making our platform suitable for both fully automated and semi-automated workflows.

user.info.profile

READ

Fields accessed: profile_web_link, bio_description, is_verified, profile_deep_link

Used to analyze the user's profile completeness and provide AI-driven recommendations for bio optimization to match their motivational content niche. For example, if a user's bio_description does not align with their posted content category, our AI will suggest improved bio copy. The is_verified flag is used to tailor onboarding guidance for verified creators.

user.info.stats

READ CORE FEATURE

Fields accessed: likes_count, follower_count, following_count, video_count

Crucial for powering the analytics dashboard in Postiz. It tracks account growth over time and measures the performance and engagement of the AI-generated motivational videos. Users rely on this data to understand which content types drive the most follower growth and to make data-informed decisions about future AI content generation.

Note to Reviewers: All requested scopes are used exclusively for the features described above. User data obtained through these scopes is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for advertising or any commercial purpose. Please refer to our Privacy Policy for full data handling details.
