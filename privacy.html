1 Data Controller

2 TikTok Data We Collect

When you connect your TikTok account to Zalyshok, we access and store the following data via the TikTok API. This data is collected only after you explicitly grant consent through TikTok's OAuth authorization flow.

Data Field API Scope Purpose
open_id user.info.basic Unique identifier to link TikTok account to Zalyshok profile
display_name user.info.basic Displayed in your Zalyshok and Postiz dashboard
avatar_url user.info.basic Displayed in your dashboard for account identification
bio_description user.info.profile AI bio optimization recommendations
profile_web_link user.info.profile Profile completeness analysis

likes_count user.info.stats Analytics dashboard — engagement tracking

We do not access, collect, or store your TikTok password, payment information, private messages, or any data beyond the scopes explicitly listed above.

4 How We Use Your Data

We use your data exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To provide and operate the Zalyshok Service, including AI content generation, video clipping, and TikTok publishing.
  • To power the Postiz analytics dashboard using your TikTok account statistics.
  • To generate personalized AI recommendations for your TikTok profile and content strategy.
  • To authenticate your identity and maintain your account session securely.
  • To send transactional communications (account updates, feature alerts, security notices).
  • To investigate and resolve technical issues or security incidents.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service.

We do not use your TikTok data for advertising profiling, training AI models for other users, or any purpose not listed here.

5 Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, our legal bases for processing personal data are:

  • Contractual necessity: Processing required to provide the Service you requested (e.g., publishing content to TikTok).
  • Consent: Processing of TikTok API data, which you authorize through TikTok's OAuth consent flow. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interests: Platform security, fraud prevention, and product improvement — balanced against your privacy rights.
  • Legal obligation: Compliance with applicable laws and regulatory requirements.

6 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

Zalyshok does not sell, rent, lease, auction, or otherwise transfer your personal data — including any data obtained through the TikTok API — to any third party for commercial purposes.

Your TikTok account data, usage behavior, and content are never used to build advertising profiles, are never shared with data brokers, and are never used to generate revenue through data monetization.

This policy applies unconditionally and does not change based on user geography or subscription tier.

7 Data Sharing & Third Parties

We share your data with third parties only in the following strictly limited circumstances:

  • Postiz: Your TikTok open_id, display name, avatar, and statistical data are shared with Postiz solely to power your scheduling and analytics dashboard. Postiz is bound by a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) and is not permitted to use your data for any other purpose.
  • AI model providers: Content inputs (scripts, prompts) you provide are processed by AI providers under strict data processing agreements. These providers do not receive your TikTok identity data.
  • Cloud infrastructure providers: Our hosting and database providers process data on our behalf under DPAs. They are not permitted to access or use your data independently.
  • Legal compliance: We may disclose your data if required by a valid legal process (court order, subpoena, regulatory demand), provided we notify you where legally permissible.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the acquiring entity, subject to equivalent privacy protections. We will notify you of any such transfer.

8 Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected:

  • TikTok API data (profile info, stats): Retained while your account is active and for up to 30 days after account deletion to allow for dispute resolution.
  • TikTok API access tokens: Stored only while your TikTok account is connected. Deleted immediately upon account disconnection or revocation.
  • Generated content: Retained while your account is active, then deleted within 30 days of account deletion unless you export it beforehand.
  • Analytics data: Aggregated, anonymized analytics may be retained for up to 12 months for service improvement.
  • Legal hold: In cases where data must be retained to comply with legal obligations, we will retain only the minimum required data for the minimum required period.

9 Data Deletion Requirements

Mandatory TikTok API compliance section — Right to Erasure

In compliance with TikTok's API Data Deletion Requirements, applicable data protection laws (including GDPR Article 17 and CCPA), and our commitment to user privacy, you have the right to request the complete deletion of your TikTok-related data from our servers at any time.

What data will be deleted:

  • Your TikTok open_id and all associated profile identifiers
  • Your TikTok display name, avatar URL, and bio data
  • All TikTok statistical data (followers, likes, video count) stored in our databases
  • Your TikTok OAuth access token and refresh token
  • All TikTok content (videos, drafts) stored on our servers that were obtained or generated via the TikTok API
  • Any Postiz analytics records linked to your TikTok account

How to request deletion — 3 methods:

  1. In-app (recommended & instant): Log in to Zalyshok → go to Settings → Connected Accounts → TikTok → Disconnect & Delete Data. This immediately revokes your OAuth token and queues all TikTok data for deletion within 48 hours.
  2. Email request: Send an email to csmanagmant@gmail.com with the subject line "TikTok Data Deletion Request" and include your registered email address. We will confirm receipt within 72 hours and complete deletion within 30 days.
  3. Account deletion: Deleting your Zalyshok account (via Settings → Account → Delete Account) will automatically trigger deletion of all associated TikTok data within 30 days.

Confirmation & verification:

  • We will send you an email confirmation once your data deletion has been completed.
  • For email requests, we may verify your identity before processing the deletion to protect against unauthorized requests.
  • We will also notify Postiz to delete your TikTok data from their systems simultaneously.
Note: Deleting your TikTok data from Zalyshok does NOT delete your TikTok account or any content already published to TikTok. For TikTok account deletion, visit your TikTok account settings directly. Additionally, we may retain certain anonymized, non-identifiable records for legal compliance and fraud prevention for up to 90 days after deletion.

10 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your browser, our servers, and the TikTok API is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Encryption at rest: Sensitive data, including TikTok API tokens, is encrypted at rest using AES-256.
  • Access controls: TikTok API tokens are stored in isolated, access-controlled systems and are never exposed in logs or client-side code.
  • Token scoping: We only request TikTok OAuth scopes that are strictly required for the described features — no excess permissions are requested or stored.
  • Security audits: We conduct regular security reviews of our API integration and data handling practices.
  • Breach notification: In the event of a data breach affecting your TikTok data, we will notify you and relevant authorities within 72 hours as required by applicable law.
